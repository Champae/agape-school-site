Aims and Objectives

  • To serve as a centre of early childhood education.
  • To instill in its pupils a sense of discipline, punctuality, honesty, and dutifulness.
  • To achieve excellence in the realms of academics.
  • To provide opportunities to exercise initiative and leadership.
  • To nurture and prepare them for service to others.

Rules and Regulations

  1. Only English should be spoken within the school premises.
  2. "Cleanliness is next to Godliness." Students should be clean in body and soul.
  3. No costly ornaments are allowed. The school is not responsible for lost items.
  4. No re-examination will be conducted under any circumstances.
  5. Late comers and students with improper uniform will be penalized.
  6. Tuition fees must be paid by the 10th of every month.
  7. Students must compensate for any damage they cause.
  8. Irregular attendance, habitual idleness, insubordination, misbehavior, obscenity, or dishonesty are grounds for dismissal.
  9. Ganging, cursing, threatening, fighting, or any violent behavior is prohibited.
  10. Participation in extracurricular activities is expected from all students.
  11. Mobile phones, radios, cameras, weapons, and electronic devices are strictly prohibited.
  12. Smoking, drinking, chewing tobacco, or drug use is strictly forbidden. The school is not responsible for off-campus behavior.
  13. Students absent for more than 15 days without valid reason may be dismissed.
